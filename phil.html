Philippine Airline

Philippine Airlines (Spanish: Líneas Aéreas Filipinas, abbreviated as PAL), a trade name of PAL Holdings, Inc. (PSE: PAL), also known historically (until 1970) as Philippine Air Lines, is the flag carrier of the Philippines.[8] Headquartered at the PNB Financial Center in Pasay, the airline was founded in 1941 and is the first and oldest commercial airline in Asia operating under its original name.[9][10][11]
